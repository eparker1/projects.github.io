ABOUT GEOMAPS CONSULT.

Geomaps Consult is a firm that provides services in GIS operations, Mapping, Land Surveying, Engineering Survey, Cadastral Survey, Construction, Quanity Surveying and Electrical Works.

Firm History

In 2014, Mr. Pius Bright, a member of the Ghana Institution of Surveyors with over 12 years of experience formed Geomaps Consult. Geomaps Consult's goal is to be an individual and a company's number one choice for all its land surveying, construction and electrical needs. We can provide services in a timely and accurate manner while staying cost effective. In order to accomplish these tasks, Geomaps continually stays current with the latest technology form field equipment to software.

Advanced Technology

Geomaps Consult uses state of the art equipment such as Robotic Total Stations, E-survey GPS, level instruments, Holy-Stone drones, the DJI Mavic pro and other various data collectors. All members of the field crew have experience in the residential, commercial and industrial surveys, along with electrical, plumbing and construction layouts. Inside the office, the firm is current with the latest software available. Currently, we are using AutoCAD along with land development desktop software. Geomaps Consult has accounts with governmental bodies in order to receive all necessary research in a timely manner. The ability to create pdfs and the in house plotter has enabled projects to be delivered in any format requested.
